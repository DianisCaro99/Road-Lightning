How it works?

The jacket comes with a set of lights designed to cover different needs that the cyclist might have on the road. Firstly, to give them more visibility, the night lights will turn on automatically whenever there is low light for example when riding behind a bridge or inside a tunnel. Also in terms of effective communication, we aim to unify it, using the same system that a car has. For that the jacket has directional and brake lights, the cyclist will use a remote control located on the handlebar of the bike can indicate if they are going to turn or if they are stopping, which will ensure a clear message for all the road actors.

The jacket its 100% waterproof which ensures a comfortable ride without having to bring other elements such as raincoats. It is customizable to ensure a unique experience. Depending on your own needs and what do you like, you can choose the colour, amount of pockets (and its location), mark the jacket with your name, blood type and emergency contact and other available features, from which you can choose.
Stationary lights
Located on the jacket sleeves, they ensure communication with other road actors inform them about your next movement.
Brake lights
Located on the back part of the jacket, alert drivers and other cyclists that you are going to stop.
Visibility lights
The night lights are located in the front and in the back part of the jacket to make you visible at night and also to illuminate you the road.
